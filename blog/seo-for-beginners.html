SEO for Beginners: How to Get Your Website on the First Page of Google

Published on September 14, 2025 by Udith Babu K N

This guide will present a practical checklist of foundational SEO tasks for beginners. It will focus on the most impactful actions a website owner can take to improve their search visibility.

Your Foundational SEO Checklist:

  • Keyword Research: Perform basic keyword research to understand what your potential customers are searching for.
  • High-Quality Content: Write comprehensive, relevant content that thoroughly answers the questions your audience has.
  • On-Page Optimization: Optimize crucial on-page elements like title tags, meta descriptions, and headers (H1, H2, etc.) with your target keywords.
  • Image Optimization: Use descriptive file names and alt text for all your images to help search engines understand their content.
  • Mobile-Friendliness: Ensure your website is fully responsive and provides a seamless experience on all devices, especially mobile phones.
  • Page Speed: Make sure your website loads quickly, as this is a critical ranking factor for both user experience and search engines.
