Affiliate Marketing for Beginners: How to Earn Passive Income

Published on September 14, 2025 by Udith Babu K N

This guide will explain the affiliate marketing business model in simple terms: a brand provides unique tracking links to its partners (affiliates), who then promote the brand's products or services. When a sale is made through one of these links, the affiliate earns a commission.

The article will outline the necessary steps for a beginner to get started:

  1. Choose a niche or topic of interest.
  2. Select a platform to build an audience (e.g., a blog, YouTube channel, or social media profile).
  3. Find and join relevant affiliate programs.
  4. Create valuable content that naturally incorporates affiliate links, such as product reviews or comparisons.
  5. Always disclose the affiliate relationship to maintain trust with the audience.
