How to Create a Mobile Marketing Strategy for 2025

Published on September 14, 2025 by Udith Babu K N

This article will provide a framework for developing a modern mobile marketing strategy.

The structure will follow these key steps:

  1. Define and understand the mobile customer by creating buyer personas.
  2. Set clear and achievable goals for the mobile strategy.
  3. Choose the most appropriate mobile channels to reach the audience, such as SMS/MMS messaging, app push notifications, or mobile-optimized email.
  4. Ensure the business website is mobile-friendly with a responsive design.
  5. Optimize content and search strategies for the rise of voice search on mobile devices.
