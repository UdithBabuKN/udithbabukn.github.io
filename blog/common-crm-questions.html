10 Common CRM Questions Every Sales Manager Asks

Published on September 16, 2025 by Udith Babu K N

This article will address common questions about CRM from a sales management and business owner perspective. The Q&A format will tackle strategic and operational concerns, including:

  • Can this CRM platform scale as our business grows?
  • How do we migrate our existing customer data into the new system?
  • How can we ensure our sales team consistently updates and uses the CRM?
  • What are the most important reports and dashboards we should be monitoring?
  • How secure is our customer data within this platform?
